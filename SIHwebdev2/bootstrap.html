How it

Works ?

-STEP-BY-STEP e-waste MANAGEMENT PROCESS-

  • → Have an electronic device(such as mobile phone,laptop, television set) you don't use anymore?
  • →Visit the website, Sign up and then sign in(so that we can learn about your current location).
  • →Search for the nearest e-Waste management centres; Select any one of the centre according to your convinience.
  • →Staff from the centre will collect your e-Waste from your doorstep. you will be incentivized(by earning credits points) for your contribution in greener planet.
  • →The collected e-Waste will be packed and sent to recycling facility for safe and responsible processing.
