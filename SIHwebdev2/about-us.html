WELCOME TO OUR WEBSITE!


The primary objectives of our website are to provide a user-friendly platform that enables individuals and businesses to easily locate nearby e-waste recycling and disposal centers. It aims to promote responsible e-waste management by offering comprehensive information on recycling options, accepted items, operating hours, and any associated fees. Additionally, the website strives to raise awareness about the environmental importance of proper e-waste disposal and encourages the sustainable reuse and recycling of electronic devices, ultimately contributing to a cleaner and more eco-conscious society.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How can I find e-waste recycling facilities near me?
A: You can use our facility locator tool on the homepage to search for e-waste recycling centers by entering your location or ZIP code.
Q: What types of electronics can I recycle at these facilities?
A: Most e-waste recycling facilities accept a wide range of electronics, including computers, smartphones, TVs, printers, and more. Check with the specific facility for a detailed list.
Q: Are there any fees for dropping off e-waste at these facilities?
A: Some facilities may charge a fee for certain items or large quantities of e-waste. It's a good idea to contact the facility in advance to inquire about any fees.
